KisanMitra is a crowdfunding platform, started to help farmers and encourage natural farming. Every year atleast one farmer suicides due to debt related problems, when we are indebted to them for life, they lose their precious life and their families future due to debt. There is nothing in the world, through which we could repay our debts to them, but it is in our control to make farmer debt free and KisanMitra is one such platform through which we can achieve this.

Through KisanMitra we can fund farmer for cultivation. When cultivation goes well, we will get unadulterated healthy natural product for a much better price.

KisanMitra doesn't stop in funding farmer, it encourages them to farm right, KisanMitra support only farmers who follow natural farming. Natural farming doesn't only provide healthy product for our next generation, preserve the environment for generations to come
